Methodology

Stories are selected where military activity, environmental disruption, and public or ecological health outcomes visibly intersect — toxic exposure at bases, conflict-driven pollution and disease, climate impacts on military planning, and related legacy contamination. Each entry links to its original source. The archive and manual additions are stored only in this browser's local storage; use Export/Import to move your archive between devices.

Editorial floor: relevant stories from Democracy Now!, The Intercept, and Dropsite News are always carried in the Email Brief, independent of their circulation score — marked with a 📌 Always in Brief badge on the story card. This is a fixed rule in the code (see PRIORITY_SOURCES), not an editable setting on the page itself.
